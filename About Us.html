CampusCycle

Closing the loop on campus stuff — buy, sell, reuse, all within Singapore's university community.

Who We Are & Why We Exist

CampusCycle is a student-run platform created specifically for university students in Singapore. We saw how much good stuff — textbooks, gadgets, bikes, furniture, clothes — gets thrown away or sits unused at the end of each semester.

So we built CampusCycle to make it super easy for students to buy cheap, sell fast, and give away items within the campus circle — keeping money in students' pockets and things out of landfills.

It's not just a marketplace. It's a small way to make campus life more sustainable, affordable, and connected.
