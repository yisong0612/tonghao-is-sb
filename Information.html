Recycling Information

Quick guide for sorting waste on campus.

Paper

  • Books, clean paper, cartons
  • No food stains

Plastic

  • Bottles, containers (clean & dry)
  • Remove caps if needed

Metal

  • Cans, small metal items
  • Rinse before recycling

General Waste

  • Food waste
  • Tissues, dirty packaging

Tip: If you are not sure, keep it as general waste to avoid contaminating recycling bins.
